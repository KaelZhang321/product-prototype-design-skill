# Traditional Admin Checklist

Use this file as the final visual QA checklist for ordinary backend pages.

If the answer to multiple items is "no", the page is not done.

## 1. Overall Impression

- Does the page look like a traditional backend management system?
- Does it avoid looking like a marketing page, analytics hero page, or designer portfolio layout?
- Is the shell quiet and operational rather than expressive?

## 2. Shell

- Is there a clear top system bar?
- Is the left navigation narrow and orderly?
- Is the content area clearly separated from navigation?
- Is there a compact tabs or breadcrumb strip when appropriate?

## 3. Density

- Are controls compact enough for operational use?
- Is the information density appropriate for a business admin page?
- Are there any oversized buttons, titles, or cards that break the traditional-console feel?

## 4. Query Area

- Does the query toolbar read as one compact operational block?
- Is the search field the dominant input when a search task exists?
- Are filter controls visually aligned and similarly sized?

## 5. Table

- Is the table real rather than text-faked?
- Are headers, rows, and cells structurally clear?
- Are column widths intentional instead of evenly compressed?
- Does the operation column use compact text actions or restrained small controls?

## 6. Pagination

- Is pagination visible?
- Is it placed naturally at the bottom-right or standard admin footer area?
- Does it look like standard list pagination rather than a floating widget?

## 7. Overlay States

- For add/edit, is the base query page preserved underneath?
- Does the mask cover the full page?
- Is the drawer topmost and clearly readable?
- For delete, is the confirmation centered and standard-looking?

## 8. Styling Restraint

- Are corners small enough?
- Are borders light and consistent?
- Are shadows minimal?
- Is color usage restrained and operational?

## 9. Negative Check

The page should not contain these unless explicitly requested:

- hero banner
- oversized KPI cards dominating the page
- gradient-heavy background
- very large corner radius
- loud sidebar branding
- decorative motion-first layout ideas
